About

I am a Senior Data Scientist at KoffeeCup, previously at SEGA Europe (2018-2024). I hold an MSc in Speech & Language Processing from the University of Edinburgh.

Outside of work, I am an active competitive quizzer. I was a member of the Edinburgh University Quiz Society and part of the 2018 University Challenge team that reached the semi-finals. I have represented Scotland at international quizzing events, including the World Quizzing Championships in 2017 and 2018, Quizzing Olympiad (2024), and Celtic Nations Quizzing Championships (2019-2024).
